I’m heavily involved in Elizabethtown College’s Society of Women Engineers (SWE) chapter. We have two major annual engineering based events that allow us to give back to the community: Toy Hack-A-Thon and Introduce a Girl to Engineering Day. The organization also has national and regional conferences which provide inspirational and innovation presentations.
